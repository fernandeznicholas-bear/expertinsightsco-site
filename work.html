Melania Cotton Candy

A woman-owned, El Paso live cotton-candy art experience. We took their bookings out of the DMs and gave them a storefront built around what makes them special — their characters.

The shift

From Instagram DMs to a real online home.

Before

Every quinceañera, birthday, and corporate order negotiated by hand in Instagram DMs — no set prices, no deposits, no calendar.

After

A branded storefront where customers browse packages, pick a date, and pay a deposit online — with confirmations sent automatically.

The characters are the brand, so the site is built around them: their packages, their colors, their voice — designed by hand, not dropped into a template.

What we built

A Signature build, end to end.

Hand-designed pages

Custom sections for every occasion they serve — birthdays, quinceañeras, corporate events, fundraisers — each built around their characters and brand.

Booking engineered

Durations, package variants, 20% deposit collection, and shared-capacity logic so two events can't double-book the same team. Branded confirmations and 24-hour reminders included.

The details that matter

Professional email on their own domain, a click-to-chat button to their phone, a content-accuracy pass on every price, and a recorded owner's training so they can run it themselves.
